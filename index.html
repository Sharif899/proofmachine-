Choose your trust level

    

    


      

        
TEE

        
Trusted Execution Environment

        

          Hardware attestations prove the enclave ran approved, unmodified code.
          The node operator cannot see, log, or tamper with your prompts.
          Negligible overhead — ideal for production LLM workloads.
        

        

          
PerformanceExcellent

          

        

        

          
Trust LevelHigh

          

        

        

          
Compute Overhead~0%

          

        

        

          Best for →
          Verifiable LLM chat, privacy-sensitive workloads, AI agents, compliance audit trails.
        

      


      

        
ZKML

        
Zero-Knowledge ML Proof

        

          A zero-knowledge proof is generated alongside inference, mathematically
          proving a specific model produced a specific output. Cryptographic
          certainty — but 1,000–10,000× overhead vs standard execution.
        

        

          
PerformanceSlow

          

        

        

          
Trust LevelMaximum

          

        

        

          
Compute Overhead1000–10000×

          

        

        

          Best for →
          DeFi risk models, on-chain ML, high-stakes decisions requiring cryptographic proof.
        

      


      

        
Vanilla

        
Signature Verification Only

        

          Signature verification confirms the response origin, but provides
          no cryptographic proof of correct execution. Zero overhead.
          Results are not independently verifiable.
        

        

          
PerformanceMaximum

          

        

        

          
Trust LevelLow

          

        

        

          
Compute OverheadNone

          

        

        

          Best for →
          Prototyping, internal tools, non-critical workloads, content recommendations.
